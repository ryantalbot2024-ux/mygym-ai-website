FAQ

Is this replacing coaches?

No. MyGym.AI scales coaching judgment responsibly. It explains decisions and adapts plans so more people can train with better guidance.

How is this different from other AI workout apps?

MyGym.AI is built around aggregated coaching decisions and transparent reasoning (“Why this workout?”), not opaque templates or single-philosophy plans.

Do I need a wearable?

No. Wearables may be supported later, but the core progression engine works with simple training feedback and logging.
