What you contribute

Scenario prompts: “Athlete stalls for 3 weeks — what do you change first?”

IP and ethics

No program copying

We do not ingest or reproduce your programs, templates, or client plans.

Abstracted decisions

Your decisions are anonymized and aggregated into decision patterns with confidence scoring.

Coach-respectful by design

Users do not receive “Coach X’s plan.” They receive explainable logic informed by many coaches.
